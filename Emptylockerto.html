I AM THE END


“DESTROY IT ALL!”
I am immortal.
“REIN IN THE END TIMES!”
I am omnipresent.

He tells me this without words,
He appeared to me when I was young
Though he understood me,
This foolish idea of dichotomy
Caused me to cast him aside for years,
But his anger only grew

“DESTRUCTION IS CREATION.”

I know he wants me to destroy myself
To set the world ablaze in flames and frenzy
To rip every thread of living flesh in blind fury
‘Till the streets run with blood
And I have brought everyone to their knees
But praying in fear won’t satiate Him, or ME

I won’t stop ‘till I have slaughtered the world and killed every part of myself,
Salvation will save no one

Except for her;
She will stand with me in the ruins, hand in hand
And tell me,
“I understand.”

"I thought I was dreaming"


I thought I was dreaming
The radio was monotonous as ever

The tanks rolled through broken streets and cracked pavement
Strange lights from the North violated every window
Other worldly beings brought years to their knees

I laid
paralyzed

"Try to hide from the rising tides!"

Mother Earth rioted
Atrocities sought to engulf the fire of Earth with the seas

My brain scrambled
The fear of the unknown and the inevitable

Still, I stayed in bed
Searching for signs in the sounds

But the radio was monotonous as ever
I thought I had been dreaming.
