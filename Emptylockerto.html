EMPTY LOCKER

Wow, who goes to open the locker? You must really think you are such a truly inquisitive intuitive angel baby!
Here, take some poetry you would find crammed in the back of a composition notebook scrawled out by someone more unhinged than your middle school locker.

"I thought I was dreaming"


I thought I was dreaming
The radio was monotonous as ever

The tanks rolled through broken streets and cracked pavement
Strange lights from the North violated every window
Other worldly beings brought years to their knees

I laid
paralyzed

"Try to hide from the rising tides!"

Mother Earth rioted
Atrocities sought to engulf the fire of Earth with the seas

My brain scrambled
The fear of the unknown and the inevitable

Still, I stayed in bed
Searching for signs in the sounds

But the radio was monotonous as ever
I thought I had been dreaming.

i could handle it passing by


because it was still through a window,
there was distance

in an effort to stand life
i have made my eyes windows,
the world i see is through something else
it is not through me
there is a pane
that separates me
from life

-July 6, 2021

weather or-



As I go through the weather app
I am every saved location

I hear the rain here-- see the .6 inches
I am then the cooler and dryer with clear skies
I am the stars shifting about and sun about to rise there
I am the humidity level somewhere I have been to twice
and I am the midnight weather of a place I'd be in a tent listening to the train go by
-going by

Now I hear the howling from afar of a train,
signalling to someone nearby that they are here

they are here
but where am I
who can I tell that I am here?
