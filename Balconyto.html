Day 1
4/25/22: Today, bad things swirled around and were pinned down by something of reality.

I watched Donnie Darko yesterday. There was some very abhorant and unexplored racism in the film? I don't remember it being that jarring the first time I watched it, but the racism and storyline with that character is never really fully flushed out or acknowledged outside of the character being a possible foil for Donnie's younger sister and having a crush on Donnie? I feel like the movie was also more shallow than I remember. I kept waiting for it to dig its teeth into something, but it felt like the whole movie was floating on water.

I am feeling very doomy today. I did a lot of coding and feel actually motivated to sit down and work on this bad boy (even though a theme for Empty Halls seems to evade me still-- I enjoy the gothic, almost unfunctionally absurd complicatedness going on right now). I feel more connected to people, but being more bound to people and this reality in general just makes me feel kinda heavy.

I tried to clean the old battery spillage in my Casio keyboard and I bought a bunch of new D batteries, but I don't know if I can revive the keyboard this time.

Tomorrow I am hoping my chest won't feel so heavy. But saying "heavy" reminds me of Florence+the Machine's song "Heavy In Your Arms" with one of my favorite lines,
