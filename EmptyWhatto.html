Empty What?


What is Empty Halls? Why are halls EMPTY or filled with the static rotation of the same with different dates? If I could capture the feeling of standing in my elementary middle school hallway years after the fact as I stared down the white and beige hall enveloping and unfurling-- shifting to consume me in every scuff mark that suggested I or at least someone had walked, laughed, thought, cried down these halls. Yet every unfurling sense of what I had or hadn't been here was constantly shifting along the overlap of time, the hall breathing, buckling, beckoning like some Eldritch abomination hall of Coraline.
It seems sometimes we are all passing through various physical spaces and realms, as well as through various abstractions. Why not blog about it?
